**Executive Excel Dashboard 
Sales Analysis of Blinkit** 

I am excited to share with you my Excel Dashboard using which I did a sales analysis of Blinkit in real time!

**Business Requirement:**
The purpose of this project was to analyze sales of Blinkit, customer service delivery, and inventory levels. Main insights were gotten through different KPIs and charts in Excel.

**KPI's Requirements:**

Total Sales: Revenue from all items sold ($1,200,000.00) 
Average Sales: Average revenue in dollar from a sale ($141) 
Number of Items Sold: Total sold unique items ($8,523) 
Average Rating (4.0)

**Insights From The Dashboard:**
Outlet Performance: Sales Analysis by the Outlet Type and their Sizes 
Item Type Distribution: Major Categories filter out the Top items by Sales
Regional Sales Analysis: Geography coverage of the outlets and sales
Customer Trends: Rating and purchase trends
